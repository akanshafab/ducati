Join a big family: Become a DOC member!

Imagine the warmth of a big family and the bright colours of flags from different countries coming together.

Imagine enjoying new experiences, exploring breathtaking landscapes, participating in exclusive events and spending unique moments riding your bike in the company of those who share your passion.

Imagine a large community that every day chooses the red of Borgo Panigale to colour its days.

Now you can stop imagining. All this already exists.

Join a Desmo Owners Club and start making your passion a source of pride!

The world of Clubs is waiting for you!

Search for the club nearest you and enter directly with the President who can tell you what it means to join the Ducatisti family. Create your profile or log in to MyDucati and discover all the advantages of the world of Club

Being a DOC member Membership Kit Promotions Great deals
  • Become part of the DOC world
    Being a DOC member means being a member of a large community that shares your passion for Ducati. By joining a Desmo Owners Club you will also enjoy many advantages and benefits designed especially for you.
    Find out more
  • Show off your passion!
    Your fun begins from the moment you join a club! As an official DOC member, you will receive patches, pins and other official gadgets to proudly show off your membership in the world of Desmo Owners Clubs.
    Find out more
  • Experience the Borgo Panigale as a DOC member!
    As an official DOC member, you can experience and learn more about the history of Ducati at the Museum and visit the Factory to discover how the Reds of Borgo Panigale are made, walking directly along the production line, all free of charge.
    Find out more
  • Access to exclusive events!
    By becoming a DOC member you will have access to special deals designed just for you. Participate in DRE riding courses, trade shows and events. Access services affiliated with Ducati and buy official apparel with special discounts.
    Find out more
