Road Academy

Take Control!

Climbing aboard a new Ducati, equipped with the most cutting-edge technology, to improve your riding ability and learn from the professionals.

This is DRE Road, the course designed both for beginners who want to raise their awareness, improving the level of safe riding, and for all those who want to test ride the Ducati model range (except the Panigale family) and take full advantage of the benefits offered by new technologies.

Ducati will provide its own bikes for each participant and the possibility to rent technical apparel.

Level: from beginner to expert

The 2020 Road Academy calendar is over. Stay updated on these pages to find out the news and dates for next season.

Theory

Dedicated to mastering full bike control, the theoretical introduction provides all the technical information for safe, conscious road riding, as well as secrets with which to perfect your riding ability and exploit the advantages offered by new technologies.

The course lasts one day, each session having a maximum of 30 people who will participate in different types of activities: the first is spent in the paddock, checking position in the seat and braking and a more dynamic part on the track with the aim of refining trajectories, insertions, apexes and travel time.

Pratical

Many exercises to test out your new knowledge and perfect your technique.

Inside the paddock at first, with exercises designed to perfect riding position, bike balance and emergency braking.

Then on track, where the focus is on trajectories, shifting, apexes, turning, and electronic control functions including ABS Cornering.

Bike

The DRE Road course gives you the chance to test ride the new models of the Ducati range and experience all the latest technological innovations of the Diavel, XDiavel, Multistrada, Monster, Hypermotard e Supersport and Streetfighter families.

Participation in the DRE Road Course is open only and exclusively to those who are in possession of the license A without limitations of displacement.

Location

Used by riders, technicians and staff, the Modena Circuit is the ideal circuit for test riding and dedicated courses.

Divided into sections during the practical sessions, it allows for the simulation of various road riding conditions. Come the end of the day, the full track provides the ideal context for track riding and enjoyment in total freedom and maximum safety

Equipment

The required apparel consists of a leather suit, a back protector, a full-face helmet, gloves and boots.

Those without the required items will be able to hire technical apparel when purchasing the course, subject to availability.
